[ 03 ] — The Mirrored Path

Systems-First Thinking

The Mirrored Path is a framework centred on self-reflection, identity alignment, and meaningful engagement — principles that translate directly into modern branding and consumer trust.

Most creators approach monetisation as a feature. "I'll add a Patreon." "I'll sell a course." These are tactics, not systems. Systems thinking asks: How does attention flow through my ecosystem? Which segments have the highest lifetime value? What is the psychological contract with each audience tier?
